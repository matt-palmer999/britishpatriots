<h2 style="font-family:'Playfair Display',serif;font-weight:900;font-size:36px;margin-bottom:6px;text-align:center;">About The Daily Patriot</h2>
<p style="font-family:'Barlow Condensed',sans-serif;font-size:13px;letter-spacing:.15em;text-transform:uppercase;color:#B8860B;text-align:center;margin-bottom:30px;">British News. Patriotic Voice.</p>

<p>The Daily Patriot was founded in 2024 by a group of concerned citizens who believed that the truth was out there, that somebody had to tell it, and that nobody else was going to. We were right on all three counts.</p>

<p>We are Britain's most patriotic news source. This is not a boast. It is a statement of fact that we are prepared to defend at considerable length, and have been, since founding.</p>

<p>Our editorial team is led by Gavin Bacon, Editor-at-Large, who has been covering the slow collapse of common sense in Britain since before it was fashionable to do so. He was not wrong then. He is not wrong now.</p>

<p>We cover politics, culture, the EU, China, the BBC, the National Trust, local councils, and anything else that has gone wrong since approximately 1997. We publish when there is something worth saying. We do not publish for the sake of it. Unlike some.</p>
